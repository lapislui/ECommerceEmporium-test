Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). Free standard shipping is available for orders over $75.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for more details.

How do I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible to make changes.
